Interests

Baking banana bread is a favorite of mine because it mirrors how I approach data analysis: carefully measuring inputs, adjusting variables, and refining the process until the final result is both clear and impactful.

Just as small changes in ingredients can affect texture and flavor, small changes in data can reveal entirely new insights. I enjoy turning raw ingredients—or raw datasets—into something meaningful, whether that’s a finished loaf or a clean, visually compelling data story.
